Transformational Leadership,

Drawing from Bass's (1985) research, it emphasizes the importance of inspiring followers through a strong vision, intellectual stimulation, and personalized attention. During our charity event simulation, my team and I incorrectly designated our initiatives as transformational, believing we were instigating meaningful change. However, we were actually utilizing Transactional Leadership: we established clear tasks, offered incentives for their completion, and concentrated on performance metrics.
